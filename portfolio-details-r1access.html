Product Overview

R1 is a leading provider of technology-driven solutions that transform the patient experience and financial performance of healthcare providers.

Introducing a Patient Access solution, a seamless blend of scheduling, registration, clearance, intake, and payment functionality, all thoughtfully crafted into a single, user-friendly experience.

With Patient Access, patients can effortlessly schedule appointments, complete registrations, navigate clearance processes, provide intake information, and make payments, all within one welcoming self-service platform. This not only streamlines their experience but also enhances efficiency for your healthcare facility. Say goodbye to cumbersome paperwork and hello to a patient-centric approach that prioritizes convenience and satisfaction.
